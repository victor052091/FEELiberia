Our Mission


Our mission is to foster the cause of education so that every citizen of Liberia irrespective of gender, socio-economic st atus, location or ethnicity can acquire literacy and education to enrich their lives thereby empowering themselves to cont ribute to the cause of their family, community and country.

Our Vision


We envision new communities where every citizen has the literacy and employable skills that are imperative for one to lead a healthier, fuller and better life.


Aims & Objectives


Our aim and object is to identify underprivileged, vulnerable, disadvantaged and marginalized children, young adults and families and to equip them through education and training with needed literacy, educational and job related skills to make them employable, well-informed and productive members of their communities and country as a whole.
