Goals:

Our goal is to:

✔ Train teachers to be able to use best practices in the delivery of instruction in schools in Liberia
✔ To develop instructional materials for secondary schools in Liberia
✔ Provide financial and emotional support to disadvantaged children and young adults to help them foster their education
✔ Provide resources to schools, educational establishments and communities to reduce the burden on impoverish people and communities
✔ To run adult literacy program form young adults
✔ Mentor at risk students especially adolescent female and disadvantage children to reduce drop up rates
✔ Provide remediation for students not passing WAEC and other examinations
✔ Support literacy in Liberia especially for marginalized sectors of the population
✔ To advocate for education and educational opportunities for Liberian children and young adults
